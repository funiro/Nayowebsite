Christmas for the Elderly

25 DEC 2025
Nancholi Community Center
10:00 AM - 4:00 PM

About the Event

Join us for a heartwarming celebration as we bring the joy of Christmas to our elderly community members. This special event is designed to create meaningful connections and spread holiday cheer among our senior citizens who often feel isolated during the festive season.

Event Highlights

  • Festive lunch and refreshments
  • Live music and entertainment
  • Gift distribution
  • Health check-ups
  • Interactive activities and games
  • Photo booth for memorable moments

How You Can Help

We welcome volunteers and donations to make this event a success. Here's how you can contribute:

  • Volunteer your time to assist with event activities
  • Donate gifts or essential items for the elderly
  • Sponsor a meal or refreshments
  • Help with transportation for elderly attendees
